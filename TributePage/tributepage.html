Hans Zimmer

One of the most greatest composers of our time.

List of some of his most recognizable compositions.

  • Zimmer was asked to compose for the 1994 film The Lion King
  • Zimmer composed for three of the Pirates of the Caribbean (Dead Man's Chest, At World's End, On Stranger Tides).
  • Zimmer co-composed for The Dark Knight and composed the rest of the trilogy.
  • Zimmer scored for the award-winning film Interstellar
  • Zimmer won his second Academy Award for Best Original Score when he composed the score for the film Dune
